Lessons/Activities


We have a numerous amount of lessons, the basics like Mathematics, English but we also have Science, Re (Religious Education), Art/Design, P.E, History and Geography.

We also have days out and go on trips very often. If you want your child to attend these trips all you have to do is fill out the form that we provide along with paying the small fee.

We also have activity days where there will be no lessons just fun activities like Lego building, Knex building, arts and crafts and many more.
