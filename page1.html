About Us

Arrows Up Primary School prides ourselves to offer the best learning experience for your child or children. In lessons we have fun and interactive tasks and activities to make your child feel comfortable while also building confidence. We have a wide verity of foods for all religions and the option to bring in pack lunches also. We have multiple playgrounds suitable for all ages with teachers monitoring them during playtime. The teachers we have are second to none as they are kind, caring and want the best for the children.
